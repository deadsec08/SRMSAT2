About SRMSAT

SRMSAT is a student-run satellite team at SRM Institute of Science and Technology. Our primary mission is to foster interest in space technology and provide a platform for students to gain hands-on experience in the design, fabrication, and operation of nanosatellites.

Our first satellite, SRMSAT-1, was successfully launched in 2011, and we are continuously working on new projects to contribute to the scientific community. We focus on various domains including electronics, computer science, mechanical engineering, and management.

Join us on our journey to explore the cosmos and innovate for the future.
