About the SRMSAT-2 Project

SRMSAT-2 is the ambitious successor to the original SRMSAT, which was successfully launched in 2011. This project is driven by the students and faculty of the SRM Institute of Science and Technology, aiming to push the boundaries of space research and nanosatellite capabilities.

Our vision is to foster a culture of innovation and provide a platform for students to gain hands-on experience in space technology, from design and development to launch and operations.
